What is BookWorm?

BookWorm is a class project designed to show mastery of HTML, CSS, JavaScrips, Jquery, AJAX and other coding languages. It is also an attempt to share my love of reading with the world by enabling everyone to quickly find and purchase books from their favorite authors or find books they may have heard about. My hope is that people will be willing to explore more books when they have an easy way to do so!

How to Use BookWorm

Using BookWorm is as easy as entering an author or book into the search bar below. If an author is entered, up to 40 of their books will be returned. If a title is entered, only one book with that title will be returned. Once anything has been returned, you can click on any book cover to see more information about that book.
